# SphereWalker and ROS
The main reason for impementing ROS onto SphereWalker is for automation purposes. The idea is that SphereWalker will one day be multi-planetary and if that is the case it will need to move autonomously.
I am planning on implementing ROS2 Humble onto SphereWalker. So far I have created an URDF for SphereWalker and am now working on a "launch" directory.
At the moment we are also experiencing some issues coding SphereWalker to turn. Our plan is to get ROS2 on it and then simulate and optimize both the forward motion and the turning in Gazebo.
